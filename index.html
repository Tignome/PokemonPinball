How to Play

Fill each elemental badge by hitting its matching shot. Three hits lights a badge and boosts your multiplier. Light all eight to claim the Champion Bonus!

  • Spacebar – Launch ball / advance prompts
  • Left Shift – Left flipper
  • Right Shift – Right flipper
